Hi, I'm Sam.
I'm a recently graduated University of Washington student with a Bachelors of Science in Informatics. I am driven by elegant design and see the beauty in complex systems that can perform at scale.
My skill set is diverse, with experience in UI, UX, front-end web & app development, and advanced back-end database development. I am a quick learner that is sure to perform in any environment I am placed in.

As a UW student I designed and populated MS SQL databases, designed UX flows for mobile apps, built fully functional Android applications, and conducted real-world research studies. My capstone was the development of a mobile app/service that allows people to quickly report city issues using computer vision.
I have interned at two separate tech consulting companies, where I learned about their business culture and how my skills could be used in the field. While there I assisted in a number of projects mainly involving MS SQL Server, Excel, and PowerBI.

I hope to leverage my skills to provide the best possible experiences through the creation and optimization of excellent systems. The Information School at UW is heavily focused on human interaction with data and technology, and I want to follow in a natural continuation of that passion. Feel free to take a look around the site to get an idea of the kind of work I've done in the past.
