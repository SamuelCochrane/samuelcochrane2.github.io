Beauticity
App Mockup

An app to modernize the 311 service request system.

Final project for INFO 200 - Introduction to Informatics.

The 311 Service Request system is a powerful but rarely used goverment service (similar to 911 or 411) that users can call to report civic issues such as graffiti, broken glass, or downed powerlines.

I designed an interactive mockup for a '311 app' (dubbed "Beauticity") that would allow users to leverage the capabilities of their smartphones to easily submit reports. Users could drop a GPS pin at the location of the issue, attach images, and more.
The new design would not only be easier than describing issues over the phone, but would provide more useful information to the government.

App mockup was designed in Illustrator, and made interactive through inVision.
An interactive mockup is available on inVision, give it a spin!
